Low Credit Car Finance — Answered

Questions people search before they apply.

Can I get approved for a car loan with low credit in Canada?

Yes. We work with a network of lenders that specialize in low credit, no credit, and post-bankruptcy or consumer-proposal financing. Our AI system matches your profile to the lenders most likely to approve it.

Will applying affect my credit score?

Starting a conversation with us doesn't require a hard credit pull. You can get an initial approval-odds read without it affecting your score.

Does a low credit car loan actually help rebuild my credit?

Lenders in our network report your payment history monthly to Equifax and TransUnion, so consistent on-time payments can help rebuild your score over time.

What if I have no credit history at all?

We work with lenders and programs built specifically for first-time borrowers and newcomers to Canada with no local credit file.

Do I need a down payment to get approved?

Not always. Down payment size affects your odds and rate, but several lenders in our network offer $0-down programs depending on your profile.
